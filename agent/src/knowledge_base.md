## What We Do

### 1. AI Strategy & Roadmapping
We sit down with your leadership team, audit your current operations, and build a
clear, prioritised AI roadmap. No fluff — just a concrete plan tied to your
business goals and existing stack.

### 2. Workflow Automation
We identify the repetitive, high-volume processes eating your team's time and
replace them with intelligent automations. Common examples: lead qualification,
document processing, customer support triage, internal reporting.

### 3. Custom AI Product Development
We design and build bespoke AI-powered products — from internal tools to
customer-facing features. This includes LLM integrations, RAG pipelines, voice
agents, and multi-agent systems.

### 4. AI Integration & Implementation
Already have a direction? We plug in and execute. We integrate AI capabilities
into your existing product or infrastructure — cleanly, securely, and at speed.

### 5. Team Enablement & Training
We run hands-on workshops and embed with your team to build internal AI fluency.
The goal is to leave you self-sufficient, not dependent on us forever.

## Our Process

We run a focused 4-phase engagement:

1. **Discovery (Week 1):** We learn your business, goals, constraints, and current
   tech stack. We identify the highest-leverage AI opportunities.

2. **Strategy (Week 2):** We deliver a prioritised roadmap with clear ROI estimates,
   risk flags, and a recommended starting point.

3. **Build (Weeks 3–8+):** We execute on the agreed scope — iterating fast, shipping
   early, and adjusting based on feedback.

4. **Handover & Support:** We document everything, train your team, and offer
   ongoing support packages if needed.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**How long does a typical project take?**
A strategy sprint takes 2 weeks. A full build typically runs 6–12 weeks depending
on scope. We can often start within 1–2 weeks of signing.

**Do you work with non-technical founders?**
Yes. We handle the technical complexity. You just need to know your business and
your goals.

**Can you work with our existing tech stack?**
Almost always yes. We're stack-agnostic and have experience across Python, Node,
React, AWS, GCP, Azure, and most major SaaS platforms.

**Do you offer equity-based engagements?**
Occasionally, for the right early-stage company. It's not our default model but
we're open to conversations.

**What makes you different from a dev agency?**
We lead with strategy, not just execution. We won't build something that doesn't
make business sense. And we specialise in AI — it's not a side service for us.

**How do I get started?**
The best first step is a 30-minute discovery call. We'll ask about your business,
your goals, and where you think AI could help. From there we'll propose a path
forward.
